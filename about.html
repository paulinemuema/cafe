Our Heritage

A century of Parisian craftsmanship

Since 1892

Founded by master patissier Édouard Laurent in the heart of Paris, Belle Époque began as a humble pâtisserie where every croissant was folded by hand and every espresso pulled with precision.

In 1927, Édouard's daughter, Colette, expanded the vision to include the café culture we're known for today, blending Italian coffee traditions with French baking artistry.

Édouard Laurent, Founder

Our Philosophy

Seasonal Ingredients

We source directly from French farms, changing our menu with the seasons to ensure peak flavor and quality.

Artisanal Methods

Every croissant is still folded using the traditional 27-step process developed by Édouard in 1892.

House-Made Extracts

Vanilla, almond, and citrus extracts are made in-house using traditional infusion methods.
